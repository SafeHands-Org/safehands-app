# Creating an Account

To begin using SafeHands, create an account.

During account setup, you will be asked to choose one of the following roles:

- **Caregiver**
- **Family Member**

### If You Choose Caregiver
You will be logged in automatically and taken to the **Main Dashboard**.

### If You Choose Family Member
You will be asked to enter a **6-character family code**. This code must be provided by the caregiver in order to join the family group. After entering the code, you will be connected to the family and given access to your personal medication information.

# Caregiver Guide

## Main Dashboard

The **Main Dashboard** provides an overview of key family medication information, including:

- **Family Overview**
- **Upcoming Doses**
- **Quick Actions**

This dashboard is the main navigation hub for caregivers.

---

## Managing Family Members

### Adding Family Members

To invite a family member to your family group:

1. From the **Main Dashboard**, tap **Family** on the bottom navigation bar.
2. Tap the **Invite** button.
3. A **6-character code** will be generated.
4. Share this code with the family member you want to add.

**Important:** Invite codes expire after **24 hours**. If the code expires, you can generate a new one by repeating the steps above.

---

### Removing Family Members

To remove a family member:

1. Go to the **Family** page.
2. Select the profile of the family member you want to remove.
3. Scroll to the bottom of their profile page.
4. Tap the red button labeled **Remove from Family**.

---

## Managing Medications

### Adding a Medication

To add a new medication:

1. Tap the **menu icon (three bars)** in the top-left corner.
2. Select **Medications**.
3. Tap the **plus (+) button** in the bottom-right corner.
4. Enter the required medication information.
5. Tap **Save Medication**.

---

### Editing or Deleting a Medication

To edit or delete an existing medication:

1. Go to the family member’s profile.
2. Locate the medication you want to update.
3. Tap the **pencil icon** to edit the medication.
4. Tap the **trash icon** to delete the medication.

---

# Family Member Guide

Family members have access only to their **own medication information**.

## Main Dashboard (Family Member)

For family members, the **Main Dashboard** displays:

- Personal medication information
- Upcoming doses
- Reminders and adherence tracking (if enabled)

Family members will not have access to caregiver-only pages such as family management or medication editing for other members.
